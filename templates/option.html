College Roots

'COLLEGE ROOTS' is a single online platform including all the necessary information about Colleges/Universities. Students are generally worried about notes, books, PDFs, links and other updates regarding academics which they tend to miss generally due to busy schedule and inefficient methods of communication. Generally freshers face the problem of not being updated with the on-going stuff. Using this app, users can upload information and all students get notified as soon as a notice is published.
